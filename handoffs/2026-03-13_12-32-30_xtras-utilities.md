## Key Learnings

1. **`_serialize_terms` had a latent Rational bug**
   - `Tuple{Int,String}` couldn't store `Rational{Int}` coefficients (e.g. `-1//4`)
   - MakeTraceFree exposed this because it creates `(1/dim)` fractional coefficients
   - Fix: changed to `Tuple{Rational{Int},String}` with proper denominator formatting

2. **Contract cannot do single-factor self-contraction**
   - `g^{ab} T_{ab}` raises both indices (gives `T^{ab}`) rather than computing scalar trace `T^a_a`
   - MakeTraceFree works around this by building the trace-free expression symbolically and letting Simplify handle cancellation

3. **MakeTraceFree metric special case**
   - When expression is a single metric factor, the trace-free formula `g_{ab} - (1/dim) g_{ab} g^{cd} g_{cd}` doesn't simplify cleanly through the engine
   - Solution: early return `"0"` for single-metric-factor expressions

4. **Pre-commit hooks reformat aggressively**
   - `julia-format` and `ruff-format` modify files; always `git add -u` after first failed commit
   - Previous session lost adapter edits 3+ times to hook reversions
